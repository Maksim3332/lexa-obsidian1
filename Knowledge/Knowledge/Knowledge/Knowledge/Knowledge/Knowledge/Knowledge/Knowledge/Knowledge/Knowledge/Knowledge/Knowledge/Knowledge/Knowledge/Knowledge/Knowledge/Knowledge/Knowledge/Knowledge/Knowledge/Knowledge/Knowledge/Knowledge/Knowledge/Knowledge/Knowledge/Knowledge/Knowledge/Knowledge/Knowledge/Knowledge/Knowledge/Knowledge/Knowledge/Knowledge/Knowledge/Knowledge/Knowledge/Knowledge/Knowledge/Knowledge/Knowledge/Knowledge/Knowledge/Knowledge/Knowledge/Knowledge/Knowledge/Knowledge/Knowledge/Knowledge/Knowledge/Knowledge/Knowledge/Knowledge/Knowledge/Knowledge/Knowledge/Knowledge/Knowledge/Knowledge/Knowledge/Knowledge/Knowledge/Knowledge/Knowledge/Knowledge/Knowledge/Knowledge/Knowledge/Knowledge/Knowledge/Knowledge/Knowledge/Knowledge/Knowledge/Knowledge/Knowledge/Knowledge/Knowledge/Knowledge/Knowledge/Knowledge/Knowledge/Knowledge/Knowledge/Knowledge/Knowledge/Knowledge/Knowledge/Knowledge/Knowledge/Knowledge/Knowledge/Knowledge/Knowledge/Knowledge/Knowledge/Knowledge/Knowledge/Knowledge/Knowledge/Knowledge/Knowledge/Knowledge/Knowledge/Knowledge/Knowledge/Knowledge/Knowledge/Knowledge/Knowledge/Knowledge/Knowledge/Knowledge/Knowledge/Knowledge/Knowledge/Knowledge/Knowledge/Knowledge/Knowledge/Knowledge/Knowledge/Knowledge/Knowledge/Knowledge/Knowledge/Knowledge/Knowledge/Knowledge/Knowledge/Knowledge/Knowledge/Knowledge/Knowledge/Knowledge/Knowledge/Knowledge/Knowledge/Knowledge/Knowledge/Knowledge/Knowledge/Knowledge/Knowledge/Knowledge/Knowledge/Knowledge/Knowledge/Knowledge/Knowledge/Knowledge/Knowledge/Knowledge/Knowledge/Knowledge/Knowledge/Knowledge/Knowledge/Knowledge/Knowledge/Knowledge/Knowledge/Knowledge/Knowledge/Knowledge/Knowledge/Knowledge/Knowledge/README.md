# Lexa Knowledge Base

Central repository for all token intelligence, macro data, and trading history.

## Structure

```
~/lexa-knowledge/
├── README.md              ← this file
├── twitter/               ← Meme analysis, sentiment, virality reports
│   ├── RKC.md             ← Per-ticker LLM analysis (humor, virality, sentiment)
│   ├── FROGE.md
│   └── ... (one .md per tracked ticker)
├── dune/                  ← On-chain metrics
│   ├── macro/
│   │   └── macro.md       ← Ecosystem-wide metrics (volumes, tokens, traders, regime)
│   └── TICKER.md          ← Per-ticker on-chain data from Dune (future)
└── trading/               ← Trade execution log
    └── trades.md          ← History of all trades: time, symbol, action, price, size, PnL
```

## File Formats

### twitter/{TICKER}.md
Generated by `meme_analyzer.py` via LLM. Contains:
- Composite Meme Score (0-100), Humor, Virality, Meme Potential, Sentiment
- LLM reasoning for each score

### dune/macro/macro.md
Updated hourly by `macro-collector.py`. Contains:
- Current risk regime (conservative/moderate/aggressive/anomaly)
- Metrics table: Solana DEX volume, Pump.fun volume, Bonk volume (0 for now), tokens launched, traders
- **Только Pump.fun и Bonk** — остальные launchpad'ы (Raydium, Meteora) не учитываются
- Risk rules with TP modifiers per regime
- Day-over-day PnL tracking

### trading/trades.md
Appended by `scanner.py --trade`. One row per trade:
- timestamp, symbol, action (buy/sell), price, size, PnL, notes

## Automation

| Cron | Script | Action |
|------|--------|--------|
| `0 * * * *` | `scanner.py --hourly` | Macro collection + DB update |
| `0 */4 * * *` | `scan-memes-4h.sh` | Twitter scan + LLM narrative analysis |
| `0 0 * * *` | `scanner.py --daily` | PnL snapshot, regime evaluation |

## Macro Data → Trading Impact

Macro regime from `dune/macro/macro.md` feeds into `growth_probability`:
- **conservative** (-5%): low TP, fast exits
- **moderate** (0%): standard strategy
- **aggressive** (+5%): hold longer, higher TP
- **anomaly** (+10%): max allocation, meme season detected
